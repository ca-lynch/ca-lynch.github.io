Photo Gallery

This page is to demonstrate the ability to integrate a photo gallery using Javascript. I decided to show a few pictures from my time in the Army. I served with one unit my whole time in the Army - 1st Platoon, Alpha Company, 2-5 CAV, 1BCT, 1CD at Fort Hood, Texas. When I got to the unit we we were called the "Black Knights". After our new commander took over our name was changed to "Lancers". Our motto was "Loyalty and Courage". I got to go all over the world with this unit and met a lot of really great guys along the way. I'm still in contact with my old platoon, and I get to see some of them every now and then.
